About

What is Vitrix?

Vitrix is an open-source first person shooter game coded in Python 3. Everybody knows lots of diffent FPS shooter games, the list is longly tiresome. So, what's any different about Vitrix? Well first of all, it has a really cool name. Second of all, it is open-source! No more sneaky malware in your closed-source applications, because the code of Vitrix is fully accessible to the community! Well? What are you waiting for? Go ahead and dive into a world of Vitrix fun!
